Focus Areas for Sixth Grade

  • Connecting ratio and rate to multiplication and division
  • Understanding division of fractions
  • Extending the system of rational numbers
  • Writing, interpreting, and using expressions and equations
  • Developing understanding of statistical thinking

Math Standards by Domain

Ratios and Proportional Relationships (6.RP)

Understand Ratio Concepts

6.RP.1-3 Understand ratio concepts, use ratio reasoning to solve problems, find unit rates, use tables to compare ratios, solve unit rate problems.

The Number System (6.NS)

Division of Fractions

6.NS.1 Interpret and compute quotients of fractions, solve word problems involving division of fractions.

Compute with Multi-digit Numbers

6.NS.2-4 Fluently divide multi-digit numbers, add/subtract/multiply/divide multi-digit decimals, find common factors and multiples.

Apply Understanding of Numbers

6.NS.5-8 Understand positive and negative numbers, understand rational numbers, understand absolute value, solve real-world problems by graphing points.

Expressions and Equations (6.EE)

Apply Properties of Operations

6.EE.1-4 Write and evaluate numerical expressions, apply properties of operations to generate equivalent expressions.

Reason About Equations

6.EE.5-8 Understand solving equations as a process of answering a question, use variables to represent numbers, solve real-world problems.

Represent Relationships

6.EE.9 Use variables to represent two quantities that change in relationship to one another.

Geometry (6.G)

Solve Real-world Problems

6.G.1-4 Find area of triangles and polygons, find volume of right rectangular prisms, draw polygons in coordinate plane, represent three-dimensional figures.

Statistics and Probability (6.SP)

Develop Understanding of Statistics

6.SP.1-5 Recognize statistical questions, understand measures of center and variability, display numerical data in plots, summarize numerical data sets.
