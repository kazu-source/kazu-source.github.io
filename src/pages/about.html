About Fresh Math

Your trusted partner in K-8 mathematics education, providing free, comprehensive resources aligned with Common Core standards.

Our Story

Admin here! I currently working as an educational tutor and execution functioning coach. I received my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering back in 2019. This website is a combination of my past education and my passion to educate currently.

I have been working as a tutor and serving students for the past five years. I primarily focus on math, so this website was created as a free reference to break down current Common Core standards in America.

Our Mission

Our ultimate goal is to break down the Common Core standards and make them easily digestable for parents and students

Our Vision

My vision for this website, which is currently still under construction, is to provide free resources for parents to support their child's math journey

What We Offer

Fresh Math provides comprehensive K-8 mathematics resources designed to support students, parents, and educators:

  • Grade-Specific Standards: Clear breakdowns of math skills for each grade level, aligned with California Common Core Standards
  • Free Worksheets: Downloadable practice materials covering key concepts and skills
  • Parent Resources: Guides and tips to help parents support their child's math learning at home
  • Structured Learning Paths: Organized content that follows the progression of mathematical concepts
  • Accessible Education: All resources are completely free and available to everyone
