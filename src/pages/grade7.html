Focus Areas for Seventh Grade

  • Developing understanding of proportional relationships
  • Operating with rational numbers
  • Working with expressions and linear equations
  • Solving problems involving scale drawings and geometric constructions
  • Drawing inferences about populations based on samples

Math Standards by Domain

Ratios and Proportional Relationships (7.RP)

Analyze Proportional Relationships

7.RP.1-3 Compute unit rates, recognize and represent proportional relationships, use proportional relationships to solve problems.

The Number System (7.NS)

Apply Operations with Rational Numbers

7.NS.1-3 Add and subtract rational numbers, multiply and divide rational numbers, solve real-world problems involving the four operations.

Expressions and Equations (7.EE)

Use Properties of Operations

7.EE.1-2 Apply properties of operations to generate equivalent expressions, understand how variables represent quantities.

Solve Real-Life Problems

7.EE.3-4 Solve multi-step real-life problems with positive and negative rational numbers, use variables to represent quantities.

Geometry (7.G)

Draw, Construct, and Describe Geometrical Figures

7.G.1-3 Solve problems involving scale drawings, draw geometric shapes, describe two-dimensional figures.

Solve Real-Life Problems

7.G.4-6 Work with circles, solve problems involving angle measure, area, surface area, and volume.

Statistics and Probability (7.SP)

Use Random Sampling

7.SP.1-2 Understand representative samples, draw inferences about populations.

Draw Informal Comparative Inferences

7.SP.3-4 Compare two populations, use measures of center and variability.

Investigate Probability

7.SP.5-8 Understand probability concepts, find probabilities of compound events.
