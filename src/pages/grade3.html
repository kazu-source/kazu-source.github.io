Focus Areas for Third Grade

  • Developing understanding of multiplication and division
  • Developing understanding of fractions
  • Developing understanding of area
  • Describing and analyzing two-dimensional shapes

Math Standards by Domain

Operations and Algebraic Thinking (3.OA)

Represent and Solve Problems

3.OA.1-4 Interpret products and quotients, use multiplication and division within 100 to solve word problems.

Understand Properties

3.OA.5-6 Apply properties of operations and understand division as an unknown-factor problem.

Multiply and Divide within 100

3.OA.7 Fluently multiply and divide within 100.

Solve Problems with Four Operations

3.OA.8-9 Solve two-step word problems using four operations. Identify arithmetic patterns.

Number and Operations in Base Ten (3.NBT)

Use Place Value and Properties

3.NBT.1-3 Round numbers to nearest 10 or 100. Fluently add and subtract within 1000. Multiply one-digit whole numbers by multiples of 10.

Develop Understanding of Fractions

3.NF.1-3 Understand fractions as numbers. Recognize and generate equivalent fractions. Compare fractions.

Measurement and Data (3.MD)

Solve Problems with Measurement

3.MD.1-2 Tell and write time. Solve problems involving measurement and estimation.

Represent and Interpret Data

3.MD.3-4 Draw scaled picture graphs and bar graphs. Generate measurement data using rulers.

Geometric Measurement

3.MD.5-7 Understand concepts of area and relate area to multiplication and addition.

3.MD.8 Solve problems involving perimeters of polygons.

Geometry (3.G)

Reason with Shapes and Their Attributes

3.G.1-2 Understand categories of shapes. Partition shapes into parts with equal areas.
