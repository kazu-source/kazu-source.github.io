Focus Areas for Fifth Grade

  • Developing fluency with addition and subtraction of fractions
  • Understanding volume
  • Working with decimals to hundredths
  • Extending division to 2-digit divisors

Math Standards by Domain

Operations and Algebraic Thinking (5.OA)

Write and Interpret Numerical Expressions

5.OA.1-2 Use parentheses, brackets, or braces in numerical expressions. Write simple expressions.

Analyze Patterns and Relationships

5.OA.3 Generate two numerical patterns using given rules and identify relationships between terms.

Number and Operations in Base Ten (5.NBT)

Understand Place Value

5.NBT.1-4 Recognize place value patterns, explain patterns in powers of 10, read/write/compare decimals to thousandths.

Perform Operations

5.NBT.5-7 Fluently multiply multi-digit numbers, find quotients with up to four-digit dividends, add/subtract/multiply/divide decimals to hundredths.

Use Equivalent Fractions

5.NF.1-2 Add and subtract fractions with unlike denominators, solve word problems involving fractions.

Apply and Extend Previous Understanding

5.NF.3-7 Interpret fractions as division, multiply fractions, solve real-world problems involving multiplication of fractions and mixed numbers.

Measurement and Data (5.MD)

Convert Measurement Units

5.MD.1-2 Convert among different-sized standard measurement units, make line plots to display data sets of measurements.

Geometric Measurement

5.MD.3-5 Recognize volume as an attribute of solid figures, measure volumes by counting unit cubes, relate volume to multiplication and addition.

Geometry (5.G)

Graph Points

5.G.1-2 Use a coordinate system to represent real-world and mathematical problems, classify two-dimensional figures.
