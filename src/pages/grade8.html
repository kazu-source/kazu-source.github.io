Focus Areas for Eighth Grade

  • Formulating and reasoning about expressions and equations
  • Understanding the concept of a function
  • Analyzing two- and three-dimensional space and figures
  • Working with irrational numbers, integer exponents, and scientific notation

Math Standards by Domain

The Number System (8.NS)

Know Real Numbers

8.NS.1-2 Know rational and irrational numbers, use rational approximations of irrational numbers.

Expressions and Equations (8.EE)

Work with Radicals and Integer Exponents

8.EE.1-4 Apply properties of integer exponents, use square/cube roots, use scientific notation.

Understand Proportional Relationships

8.EE.5-6 Graph proportional relationships, analyze similar triangles.

Analyze and Solve Linear Equations

8.EE.7-8 Solve linear equations, analyze and solve systems of linear equations.

Functions (8.F)

Define and Compare Functions

8.F.1-3 Understand functions, compare properties of functions, analyze functions using graphs.

Use Functions to Model Relationships

8.F.4-5 Construct functions to model linear relationships, describe qualitative features of functions.

Geometry (8.G)

Understand Congruence and Similarity

8.G.1-5 Verify properties of rotations, reflections, and translations, understand congruence and similarity.

Understand and Apply the Pythagorean Theorem

8.G.6-8 Explain proof of the Pythagorean Theorem, apply it to find distances.

Solve Real-World Problems

8.G.9 Know and apply formulas for volumes of cones, cylinders, and spheres.

Statistics and Probability (8.SP)

Investigate Patterns

8.SP.1-4 Construct and interpret scatter plots, investigate patterns of association in bivariate data.
