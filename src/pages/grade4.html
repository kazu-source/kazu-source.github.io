Focus Areas for Fourth Grade

  • Developing understanding of multi-digit multiplication
  • Developing understanding of fraction equivalence and operations
  • Understanding geometric figures and their properties
  • Working with decimals and measurement conversion

Math Standards by Domain

Operations and Algebraic Thinking (4.OA)

Use Four Operations

4.OA.1-3 Interpret multiplication equations, multiply or divide to solve word problems, use factors and multiples.

Generate and Analyze Patterns

4.OA.4-5 Find factor pairs, identify prime/composite numbers, generate number or shape patterns.

Number and Operations in Base Ten (4.NBT)

Generalize Place Value

4.NBT.1-3 Recognize place value to millions, read and write multi-digit numbers, compare numbers.

Use Place Value Operations

4.NBT.4-6 Fluently add/subtract multi-digit numbers, multiply up to 4 digits by 1 digit, find quotients and remainders.

Extend Understanding of Fractions

4.NF.1-2 Explain and generate equivalent fractions, compare fractions with different numerators/denominators.

Build Fractions

4.NF.3-4 Add/subtract fractions, multiply fraction by whole number, solve word problems.

Understand Decimal Notation

4.NF.5-7 Express fractions with denominators 10 or 100 as decimals, compare decimals to hundredths.

Measurement and Data (4.MD)

Solve Problems with Measurement

4.MD.1-3 Know relative sizes of units, convert measurements, apply area/perimeter formulas.

Represent and Interpret Data

4.MD.4 Make line plots to display measurement data involving fractions.

Geometric Measurement

4.MD.5-7 Understand concepts of angle and measure angles.

Geometry (4.G)

Draw and Identify Lines and Angles

4.G.1-3 Draw points, lines, angles, and shapes. Recognize symmetry in two-dimensional figures.
